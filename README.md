# UK Online Retail Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes transactional sales data from a UK-based online retail company using SQL and Power BI. The objective was to identify revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, product performance, and geographic market trends through data cleaning, KPI development, and interactive dashboards.

---

## Tools Used

- SQL Server
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel Dataset

---

## Dataset

Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository

https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/352/online+retail

The dataset contains transactional sales records from December 2010 to December 2011, including customer purchases, products, quantities, prices, and countries.

## Key Business Insights

- The business generated £10.67M in revenue.
- The United Kingdom contributed approximately 85% of total revenue.
- High-value customers generated over 40% of total revenue.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of top-performing products.
- International markets represent an opportunity for growth.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase retention efforts for high-value customers.
- Expand marketing in high-value international markets.
- Reduce dependency on a single geographic market.
- Prioritize inventory planning for top-performing products.
- Improve customer identification and data quality processes.
